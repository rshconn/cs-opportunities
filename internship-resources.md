## Freshman & Sophomore Internship Resources

#### Writing a Resume
Your first stop for help making and/or revising your resume should be the career center at your school. Career centers have counselors who can go over your resume with you and help you find appropriate jobs and internships to apply to. They also offer workshops, panels, and other career opportunities. Find your career center: https://www.cuny.edu/current-students/student-affairs/student-services/career-services/campus-career-centers/.

#### First & Second Year Summer Internship Programs
Several companies have paid internship programs specifically designed for first- and second- year students. **Apply ASAP!** Their deadlines are often during the fall semester.
